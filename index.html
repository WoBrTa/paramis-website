About

Woody Taylor

I help organizations adopt generative AI in a way that is practical, governed, and tied to measurable outcomes. My work focuses on translating fast-moving model capabilities into clear operating guidance: what to do now, what to avoid, how to build internal fluency, and how to scale from early use cases into repeatable workflows.

Through Paramis, I advise leaders on AI strategy, embed with teams to stand up real workflows, and teach hands-on programs for executives, managers, and power users. My delivery is applied and outcomes-driven: role-based prompting patterns, safe-use guardrails, prompt libraries, lightweight assistants, and practical measurement so adoption becomes visible and sustainable.

Across two decades in senior leadership, I've led complex operational and technology transformations in healthcare, banking, and enterprise services. I pair this foundation with deep expertise in Six Sigma, Lean, Agile, and organizational change management, treating AI adoption as a real transformation program rather than a tool rollout.

I take a pragmatic, workforce-centered approach: equip people with real skills, embed verification habits, and build governance and metrics that let leaders steer adoption with confidence.
